Armory Access

Prototype weapons, adaptive attachments, and support gadgets ready for deployment. Tune your kit to your squad's objective before the dropship leaves orbit.

Signature Weapons

VX-99 Revenant
Bullpup assault rifle with recoil-cancel actuator and AI-assisted burst predictor. Best-in-class mid-range stability.
Tempest Coil
Charge rifle that fires compressed lightning. Pierces shields and chains to nearby targets.
Hailstorm SMG
Mag-fed submachine gun with cryo-rounds that briefly slow enemies they hit.
Aegis Pike
Hybrid spear/shotgun with deployable cover emitter when planted into ground.

Attachment Lab

Spectral Optics
Thermal-ready glass that highlights sprinting targets and outlines cloaked units.
Grav Stabilizer
Redirects recoil energy downward, enabling perfect vertical control.
Pulse Drum
Extended mag that releases sonar pulses when reloading to expose flankers.

Support Gear

Bastion Shield
Deployable barrier that curves to protect the team while letting bullets exit freely.
Specter Drone
Remote drone capable of hacking turrets and collecting intel simultaneously.
Phoenix Stim
Self-revive injector with a three-second wind-up. Use after clearing threats.
